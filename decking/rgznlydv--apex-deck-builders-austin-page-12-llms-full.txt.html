Composite vs Wood Decks: Which is Right for Your Austin Home One of the most critical decisions for any homeowner considering a new deck is choosing between composite and wood materials. Each option presents its own set of benefits and considerations, particularly in Austin’s unique environment. Traditional wood decking exudes natural warmth and beauty, perfectly complementing lush landscapes. It offers unparalleled aesthetics and allows homeowners a certain personal touch with staining and sealing options.
