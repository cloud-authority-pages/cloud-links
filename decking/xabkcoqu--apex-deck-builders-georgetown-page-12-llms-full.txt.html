Q: What types of materials are used for custom decks? A: For custom decks, the main materials include wood, composite, and PVC, each offering distinct advantages. Wood, like cedar or treated pine, provides a warm, traditional look and is cost-effective but needs periodic sealing to prevent weathering. Composite materials, blending wood fibers and plastic, deliver excellent durability, fade resistance, and low maintenance, making them popular for busy areas. PVC options are lightweight, splinter-free, and highly resistant to moisture, insects, and UV damage, ideal for high-traffic spots. In Central Texas, select based on your climate exposure, budget, and desired aesthetic—wood for charm, composites for longevity, or PVC for minimal upkeep—to ensure the deck suits your needs and lasts for years.
