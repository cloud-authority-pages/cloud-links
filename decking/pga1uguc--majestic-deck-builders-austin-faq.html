For decking, popular materials include wood, composite, and PVC, each with distinct advantages. Wood, like cedar or pressure-treated pine, offers a natural look and feel but needs regular sealing to prevent weathering. Composite decking, made from wood fibers and recycled plastic, is low-maintenance, resists rot and insects, and comes in various colors. PVC is fully synthetic, providing excellent durability against moisture, fading, and scratches. The best choice depends on your climate, budget, and maintenance preferences—wood for charm, composite for balance, or PVC for longevity. Always factor in local codes and consult a professional for installation to ensure safety and performance.
