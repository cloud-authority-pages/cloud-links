2Composite vs. Wood Decks: What Austin Homeowners Need to Know

Learn more about Apex Deck Builders Austin. While wood decks have long been favored for their classic beauty and natural appeal, they often come with a considerable list of disadvantages. Wood requires regular maintenance, including staining, sealing, and replacement of rotting boards, especially in the humid climate of central Texas. Composite decking, on the other hand, is engineered to withstand adverse weather conditions, eliminating the need for frequent upkeep.
