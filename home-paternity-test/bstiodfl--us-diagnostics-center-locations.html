4Legal vs. Personal Use Paternity Testing in Ohio

In Ohio, it's important to understand the distinction between legal and personal use paternity testing. Learn more about home paternity test. A home paternity test kit, like the ones offered through our website, provides results that are intended for informational purposes only and are not admissible in court. For legal purposes, a specific chain-of-custody procedure must be followed.
